The Frozen North (1922)

The Frozen North is a classic silent film released in 1922 in America. Buster Keaton and Edward F. Cline directed this movie. It is a comedy set in the North Pole, in which, with particular humour the main protagonist (Buster Keaton), conducts various stunts, playing the role of a bandit. A 17-minute public domain film that has become a classic of the 1920s. More information here.
